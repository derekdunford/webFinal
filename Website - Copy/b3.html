Big Burger was founded on the idea that food should be simple, satisfying, and above all delicious. Our menu is streamlined and time-tested, and if we know one thing, it's that you can't go wrong with a classic.

Big burgers are built on 1/2lb Angus beef patties served with only the freshest toppings and real cheddar cheese. Our Idaho potato sourced french fries are crisped to perfection until they achieve the optimal texture and golden color you can't find at some corporate chain.

They might call us the underdog, but never the little guy.

Dream Big, Eat Bigger.
